Brand identity for "BananaSplit", a modern app for splitting group expenses, tracking shared money, and helping
friends or groups stay financially organized.

Create a clean, warm, memorable mascot-based visual identity centered on a banana character with expressive eyes. The
banana mascot should feel friendly, clever, trustworthy, slightly playful, and emotionally likable, while still
looking polished enough for a real finance/productivity app. The style should be modern, premium, simple, and
brandable, not childish and not messy.

Visual direction:
- mascot: banana with eyes
- personality: friendly, smart, confident, approachable
- aesthetic: modern startup branding, soft fintech feel, mobile-first, rounded geometry
- color palette: banana yellow, butter yellow, golden yellow, soft cream, deep cocoa brown accents
- rendering style: clean vector-like illustration, subtle gradients, soft premium shadows, crisp silhouette, high
readability
- shape language: rounded, smooth, minimal, iconic
- mood: warm, organized, social, polished, playful in a controlled way

The mascot can subtly suggest ideas of splitting, balance, shared expenses, friendship, or money flow through shape,
pose, expression, or simple supporting elements, but the design should remain minimal and uncluttered.

Avoid:
- childish cartoon style
- overly detailed illustration
- realistic banana textures
- clipart look
- cluttered composition
- too many props
- ugly generic AI logo style
- aggressive or weird facial expressions
- crypto / cyberpunk / neon aesthetics
- purple-heavy palettes

Maintain strong brand consistency across all outputs so every image clearly belongs to the same product family.
